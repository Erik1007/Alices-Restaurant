The Home of Alice's Restaurant, with its simple and clean design language, has been a landmark in Stockholm since 1955. It was designed by the well-known architect Peter Celsing back in 1949 and was the first architect-designed pier side gas station in Sweden. With its humble and yet sleek design, this building has been the centerpiece of the inner harbor for over 70 years.

After a number of different businesses, such as tourist office, ice cream bar, cafe and pizzeria, the time finally came to transform the iconic structure into a pure restaurant and brasserie with bars and outdoor seating. The future looks as bright as a glass of champagne overlooking the calm harbor
