SIGNATURE COCKTAILS

  • PORNSTAR MARTINI

    A classic blend of sweet Absolut Vanilia vodka with tart passion fruit for a refreshingly fruity cocktail. Served with a shot of Prosecco and garnished with luxurious gold sugar to bring a touch of opulence to any occasion.

  • ESPRESSO MARTINI

    With a blend of Ketel Onevodka, Tia Maria, sugar syrup and coffee, this is a smooth, rich and creamy cocktail that oozes sophistication. Garnished with coffee beans to awaken the senses.

  • FRENCH 75

    A vibrant mix of citrus Bombay Premier Cru gin, sugar, lemon juice and Prosecco for a light and refreshing cocktail that sparkles. Garnished with a lemon twist for aromatic pleasure with every sip.

  • SALTED CARAMEL OLD FASHIONED

    Warm, salty and sweet, Woodford Reserve Kentucky bourbon whiskey meets salted caramel syrup and angostura bitters in this modern twist on an old fashioned. Perfectly paired with a side of salted caramel popcorn.

  • LONG ISLAND ICED TEA

    Ketel One vodka, Bombay Sapphire gin, Olmeca tequila, Bacardi Carta Blanca rum, Cointreau, lemon and Pepsi Max

  • MOJITO

    Bacardi Carta Blanca rum, fresh mint, fresh lime, sugar syrup and soda. Make it fruity with lychee, passion fruit,strawberry or raspberry. Swap soda for Prosecco for +1.5.

  • AMARETTO SOUR

    Combining sweet Disaronno amaretto with lemon juice, agave and angostura bitters,this cocktail is perfectly balanced for a sweet yet sour delight.
